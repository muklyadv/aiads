Frequently asked questions

What’s included in the 5-sample pack?

Five polished short-form ads tailored to a product angle, sized for TikTok/Reels/Shorts, export-ready with captions and hooks to test quickly.

Turnaround time for the first batch?

Typically 3 days for the first 10 concepts or 5 finished edits, depending on inputs and references provided.

Do you handle script, hooks, and angles?

Yes—angle ideation, hooks, basic scripts, and light CTA copy are included to move from concept to test quickly.

What if the ads need revisions?

Reasonable revisions on copy, captions, and trims are included in the pilot so tests align with positioning.

Which platforms/formats are supported?

TikTok, Meta (Reels), YouTube Shorts, plus square cuts when required; captions burned-in on request.
